Understanding the Interface

This is what the calculator looks like in it's initial state.

To start a calculation, click on (whole) number to start with.

For the non-numerical buttons, here are the uses:
  • Clicking on the Clear Button [C] deletes the current mixed fraction.

  • Clicking on the Reset Button [R] deletes the entire current expression.

  • Clicking on the Backspace Button [‚Üê] deletes the most recently entered digit of the current mixed fraction.

  • Clicking on the Sign Button (top right) changes the sign of the current mixed fraction.

  • Clicking on the Position button [Pos] moves the curser to inside the fraction, starting with the numerator.

After a Number is Entered

Here, the user has entered an 8 in the whole number position.

To enter the numerator of the fraction, simply press the position button, followed by your number.

After entering the numerator, press the position button again to focus on the denominator.
